How to Join the Umrah Lucky Draw

Our spiritual journey to Umrah begins with intention and preparation. To join the Umrah Lucky Draw, the first step is to purchase an Umrah Saving Plan. Once the saving plan is purchased, you become eligible to enter the Lucky Draw.

This is the beginning of a journey that brings your heart closer to the House of Allah.

How it works?

  • As you continue to pay your monthly installments, your Lucky Draw entries increase with every step you take, reflecting your commitment and gradually improving your chances of being selected for Umrah.
  • If your name is chosen in the draw, you will be able to travel for Umrah in the following month, turning your preparation into a blessed reality.
  • Any remaining installments after the draw will be waived off, allowing you to focus fully on your worship. This process is designed to support your spiritual journey, helping you move forward with patience, hope, and trust in Allah.

Terms & Conditions

  • The Umrah Lucky Draw is non transferable and cannot be sold or shared with another person.
  • The draw is strictly bound to the registered account holder's name.

Note: Please stay alert and protected from scams. Always rely on official communication and approved channels only.
